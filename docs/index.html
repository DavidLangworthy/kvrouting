Where should idle KV live during the think gap?

Between turns the session's KV is resident but not read. Pinning it in HBM steals slots from active work; the penalty is the duty-cycle factor 1 + I/Ts. Offloading it node-locally costs a PCIe round trip.

How big does the prefill pool get?

Discarding KV between turns means every turn is a cold recompute — O(s²) attention. Offloading means only the delta recomputes. This is the whole case for the offload tier, in nodes.

Rebalance during the think gap?

The affinity/balance tension above is only forced if you decide at admission. But between turns the session's KV sits idle with nobody waiting on it. Move it to a balanced node during the gap, over otherwise-idle fabric, and the turn arrives to a node-local reload on a balanced node — affinity's TTFT and balance's throughput.
